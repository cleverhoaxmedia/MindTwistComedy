What's Inside

Every ingredient
has a purpose.

We believe transparency is the foundation of trust. Unlike drinks that hide behind "proprietary blends," Zenergy lists every active ingredient with full disclosure. You deserve to know exactly what you're putting in your body — and why.

  • L-Theanine — The amino acid found in green tea that smooths caffeine's edge and promotes alpha-wave brain states associated with calm alertness.
  • Ashwagandha Extract — An adaptogen with centuries of Ayurvedic use, clinically shown to reduce cortisol and support steady-state energy.
  • Bamboo Silica — A natural electrolyte and antioxidant sourced from organic bamboo. Hydration that works at the cellular level.
  • Cold-Pressed Ginger — Natural anti-inflammatory with a warming, grounding quality that complements rather than masks the flavor profile.
  • Lotus Flower Extract — Rich in nuciferine, a calming alkaloid that supports parasympathetic nervous system balance during stimulation.
